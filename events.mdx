# Events Overview

View and manage all your scheduled events in one place. The Events page provides a comprehensive view of your booking history and upcoming appointments.

## Event Filtering

You can filter your events by status to easily find what you're looking for:

**Upcoming Events** - View all scheduled events that are yet to occur

**Past Events** - Review completed events and booking history

**Canceled Events** - See events that were canceled or rescheduled
