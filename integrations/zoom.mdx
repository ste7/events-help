---
title: "Zoom"
description: Connect your Zoom account with Novacal to enable automatic video conference link generation for specific event types.
---

## How to connect your Zoom account

1. Navigate to the Integrations page and go to Meetings tab
2. Click the Connect button and you will be redirected to the Zoom login page. Log in with your Zoom credentials and grant Novacal the necessary permissions.
3. Confirm Connection.

## How to remove connected Zoom account

1. Go to the Integrations page and select the Meetings tab
2. Find your connected Zoom account and click the Unlink button
