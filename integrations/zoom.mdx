---
title: "Zoom"
description: Connect your Zoom account with Novacal to enable automatic video conference link generation for booked meetings.
---

## How to connect your Zoom account

1. Navigate to the **Apps & Integrations** page and go to [Zoom](https://app.novacal.io/integrations/zoom)
2. Click the **Connect** button and you will be redirected to the Zoom login page. Log in with your `Zoom credentials` and grant Novacal the necessary permissions.
3. Confirm **Connection**.

## How to remove connected Zoom account

1. Navigate to [Apps & Integrations](https://app.novacal.io/integrations) page and then go to [Zoom](https://app.novacal.io/integrations/zoom)
2. Click the **Disconnect** button
